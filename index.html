Ram Ghat


Ram Ghat, which is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Uttar Pradesh, is believed to be the place where Lord Rama, Goddess Sita and Lord Laxman.

Gupt Godavari


This is a tiny rivulet which flows into tanks at the end of an underground cave. The source of this rivulet remains unfathomable.

Shabri waterfall


Shabri waterfall near Chitrakoot Dham, u.p is one of the most beautiful places in Bundelkhand. Shabri waterfall landscape is filled with water running over rocks, falling down from height with the loud noise.
